Dental Implant

Root Canal Treatment

Dental Implants are advanced tooth replacement solutions used when a natural tooth is lost due to decay, injury, or disease. A titanium post is surgically placed into the jawbone, acting as an artificial root to support a crown. Dental implants restore both appearance and chewing function, prevent bone loss, and feel just like natural teeth—ensuring long-term comfort and confidence.

Procedure

The entire process is painless from start to finish:

  • A thorough clinical and radiographic assessment is done to evaluate bone density and structure.
  • The damaged or missing tooth is gently extracted if not already lost.
  • A titanium implant is surgically placed into the jawbone to act as an artificial root.
  • The implant site is allowed to heal and integrate with the bone (osseointegration), usually over 3–6 months.
  • After healing, an abutment is placed on the implant to support the final crown.
  • A natural-looking ceramic or zirconia crown is custom-made and attached to restore appearance and function.
  • The procedure is typically painless with minimal discomfort and a high success rate when properly maintained.

Single Tooth Implant

Ideal for replacing a single missing tooth. A titanium post is placed into the jawbone, and after healing, a custom crown is fitted to match your natural teeth seamlessly.

Multiple Teeth Implants

Suitable for replacing several missing teeth. Implants are strategically placed to support individual crowns or a bridge, offering better stability than removable options.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation

When all teeth are missing, full mouth implants (such as All‑on‑4 or All‑on‑6 systems) provide a fixed, natural-looking solution with improved chewing efficiency and aesthetics.

Why Dental Implants Are a Preferred Choice


  • Preserves jawbone and prevents bone loss
  • Feels and functions like a natural tooth
  • Does not affect adjacent healthy teeth like bridges
  • Provides permanent, long-lasting results with proper care
  • Improves chewing ability and facial aesthetics

Unlike removable dentures, implants offer a fixed, stable solution that enhances quality of life and confidence—making them the gold standard for tooth replacement.
