Smile Designing

Self assess your smile?

– Are you self-confident when you smile?
– Are your photographs bad when taken from any angle of face?
– When you look at your teeth in a mirror, do you find any defects on your teeth?
– Do you wish your teeth were whiter?
– Do you show too many of too less teeth when you smile?
– Do you show excess gums when you smile?
– Is your gums dark in colour?
– Do you feel that your teeth are too long or too short?
– Are your teeth appearing fat and broad or thin and narrow?
– Are you satisfied with the shape and cuteness of your teeth?
– Are you having gaps between your teeth?
– From the side view do you feel that your teeth are protruding out a bit?
– Are one or a few teeth darker than the other teeth?
– Do your crowns, ceramic veneers/laminates, composite bonding, fillings etc have an artificial appearance?
– Are your teeth aligned in a uniformly curved manner?
– Are you not able to pin point exactly, what is wrong with your smile?
– Are you attracted to a model’s smile?

There are situations where you are not happy with the way you look or your smile, but you cannot pinpoint or find out exactly what is the problem and where it is. You may just know something is wrong somewhere on your face.

“Access to your smile”

Teeth shape, size, colour and position in the mouth must blend with the face and lips when smiling. The process of harmonizing these elements is known as Smile Correction.

ART OF DENTISTRY (2 to 4 visits)

DENT–ART (Art of Dentistry) is an alternative to orthodontic treatment, where teeth can be camouflaged to achieve a good smile in 2 to 4 visits within a week’s time.

  • Gap closure
  • Protruded teeth
  • Long / Short / Broad / Narrow teeth
  • Displaced / Rotated teeth
  • Crowded / Malaligned teeth
  • Excess gum exposure on smile

Even discolored, shapeless, or fractured teeth can be corrected within a couple of days.
